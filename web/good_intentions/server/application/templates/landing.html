Welcome to the Starfinder Project!

Thank you for showing interest in our efforts in collecting labeled images of our vast and amazing universe!

The Starfinder Project is dedicated to becoming a repository of labeled space images to assist machine learning models identify objects in space.

We are currently mid-development for our image collection website. As you can see it's not much of a looker quite yet, but we were too excited to get started!

For now you can help the project by uploading your labeled images via our published API!
